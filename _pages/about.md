I am a postdoctoral researcher at <a href="https://www.inria.fr/en">Inria Paris</a> in the <a href="https://sierra-mlopt.github.io/">Sierra team</a>, working with <a href="https://adrientaylor.github.io/">Adrien Taylor</a> and <a href="http://www.cmap.polytechnique.fr/~aymeric.dieuleveut/">Aymeric Dieuleveut</a>. My supervisors were <a href="https://www.control.lth.se/personnel/personnel/pontus-giselsson/">Pontus Giselsson</a> and <a href="https://github.com/sbanert">Sebastian Banert</a>.
